About Us

At ASCAR, we specialize in providing top-quality car rental services witha fleet of modern,
well-maintained vehicles. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, we are
dedicated to offering reliable, affordable, and professional rental solutions. Our mission is
to help you find the perfect vehicle for your trip—whether for business, leisure, or special
occasions—quickly and hassle-free.


We pride ourselves on our exceptional customer service, flexible rental plans, and a wide range of vehicles—from compact cars to luxury SUVs. Whether you need a car for a day, a week, or even long-term, ASCAR is here to deliver a seamless and satisfying rental experience tailored to your needs.


Our mission is to make transportation easy and accessible by offering trusted, affordable, and convenient car rental solutions for individuals and businesses alike. We aim to become the leading car rental company in Rwanda and the region, setting the standard for reliability and customer satisfaction.
